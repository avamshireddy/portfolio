Why Do I Need to Provide My Birthday?

At Facebook, your birthday helps us provide you with a better, safer, and more personalized experience. Here are a few reasons why we ask for your birthday:

  • Age-appropriate Content: We use your birthday to ensure you see content that is appropriate for your age group.
  • Account Security: Your birthday helps us verify your identity and keep your account secure.
  • Personalized Experience: We use your birthday to provide you with a more personalized experience, including birthday reminders for your friends.
  • Legal Requirements: We need to ensure that we are compliant with laws and regulations that restrict the use of our service based on age.

We value your privacy and take it seriously. For more information on how we use your data, please review our Privacy Policy.
